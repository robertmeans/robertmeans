View from tent. Incredible spot, perfectly flat, facing west with rock dome over back of tent that provided dark shade & cool temps until almost 11:00 a.m! I got up at 6:00, made coffee & absorbed the serenity. Kids & Bandit slept until after 10:00.

 

Our routine is to hike in for a couple of hours, off trail and typically straight up, until we find the perfect spot. I set up camp and then we all go back down to the stream and fill up on water. Round trip to get water this time: 1.5 hours. Behold, the most beautiful 6 pack ever.

 

Getting ready for S'mores. Note their shoes. The kids have mountain shoes & boots but prefer to hike in their water shoes. They scale up & down these mountains, with packs, like spider monkeys.
