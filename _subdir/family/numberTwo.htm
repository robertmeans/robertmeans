Wow, what a difference an epidural makes!

Samantha´s water broke at 5:09 a.m. and the midwife told us to go ahead and make our way to the hospital. After a few contractions Samantha quickly opted for the epidural. Nearly seven hours later (while we slept & watched TV) it was time to push. It took roughly 20 minutes for her to come out. All the while Samantha remained perfectly coherent and pleasant.

Everybody is doing well. I drove up to Evergreen a little while after everything had settled to pick up Stanton from our friends who had kept him all day. (Thank you Caroline & Pete!) We will spend the night in the hospital and, everything permitting, go home tomorrow.
