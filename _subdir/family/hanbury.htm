J.D. Hanbury

Rest in peace



The embodiment of kindness & a stellar example

How this name ended up in our family:

When my dad was a young boy his father (Welch Earl Means) passed away from tetanus. Years later my grandmother married J.D. He raised my hell-raising father & uncle and has somehow managed to maintain the overall friendliest demeanor of anyone, anywhere.
